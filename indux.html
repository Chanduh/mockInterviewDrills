Codesmith Technical
Interview Prep

If you are mock interviewing live with another person, your unique URL should allow your interviewer to follow along with you in your console.

Clicking the start button will randomly generate and initiate the test. All tests will have at least one reursion and one OOP challenge. Don't forget to set yourself a 50 minute timer! When you have completed a challenge, select “Next Question” to move on. Be sure to test your own code before you do so as this test will NOT be assessing your solutions for you.

Verbal questions and misc. technical communication pointers are available for you via the menu.
